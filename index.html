A Brief History of Ravenna Park
Just north of Seattle's University District neighborhood lies Ravenna Park, a lush, green space that has been home to hiking, biking, and picknicking for over a century. While today anyone can enjoy the park and its amenities, it wasn't always open to the public. In fact, Ravenna Park has a rich history that dates back to the late 1880s, when European settlers arrived in the area and began to develop the land. The land is not only historically significant, but also ecologically and culturally important to the indigenous peoples who initially called Ravenna Park home.
